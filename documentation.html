Data Management Plan for VISIONS

The VISIONS project participates in the Horizon 2020 Pilot on Open Research Data. VISIONS’s Data Management Plan (DMP) describes the types of data that will be utilized and generated during the project, the standards that will be used, how the data will be exploited and shared for verification and reuse, as well as the preservation and storage of data.

The provisions for open access to research data are described in Grant Agreement Arts 29.3 Open access to research data and 39.2 Processing of personal data by the beneficiaries. This document follows the template for the H2020 Data Management Plan v1.0 (13/10/2016).
